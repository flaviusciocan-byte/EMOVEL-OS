# Gumroad Launch Prep 001: AI Instagram Content OS for Solo Founders

## Final Product Title

AI Instagram Content OS for Solo Founders

## Short Description

Plan 30 days of offer-led Instagram content with reusable AI prompts, post templates, content pillars, launch sequences, and a weekly planning workflow built for solo founders.

## Long Description

If Instagram keeps turning into a blank-page problem, this is the system that gives your content a job.

AI Instagram Content OS helps solo founders turn their offer, audience, and product ideas into a repeatable content workflow. Instead of asking AI for random post ideas, you use structured prompts that connect every piece of content to a buyer belief, objection, pain point, proof point, or conversion moment.

This is built for founders who sell services, consulting, coaching, templates, software, courses, or digital products and need Instagram to support the business without becoming a full-time content creator.

Use the system to clarify what your Instagram content should be about, build content pillars around your offer, generate 30 days of post ideas with a business objective, turn ideas into carousels, Reels, stories, and launch posts, and review content before publishing so every post has a point.

Important: this product does not promise viral growth, revenue, followers, or algorithm outcomes. It gives buyers a practical planning system and AI prompt structure for creating more consistent, offer-led Instagram content.
