# EMOVEL Launch Stack v1

> First monetizable product built from the EMOVEL skill system.

## Overview

EMOVEL Launch Stack v1 is a premium AI-powered launch system that converts a raw product idea into the assets required for a serious first launch.

It operationalizes the Phase 3 Launch Stack goal in the EMOVEL Product Roadmap by turning the documented skills in `knowledge/skills/` into a sellable product package.

## Positioning

A premium AI-powered launch system for founders, creators, consultants, and micro-agency operators who have a useful product idea but need a clear market package before selling.

The product transforms:

- scattered notes into a launch brief
- product features into an offer
- price anxiety into tier logic
- blank-page copy into a landing page
- vague brand taste into a visual brief
- random posting into a launch content plan

## Launch Strategy

Initial launch should prioritize warm traffic, direct outreach, and founder/operator content.

Do not start with paid ads. The first launch should produce:

- 3 paying Core customers
- 1 Premium upsell
- 3 feedback notes or testimonials
- One documented case study

## Success Criteria

EMOVEL Launch Stack v1 is validated when:

- A buyer can complete the intake without hand-holding.
- The stack produces a usable offer, price, page structure, visual brief, and launch plan.
- At least 3 buyers pay for the Core offer.
- At least one buyer upgrades to Premium or asks for implementation support.
- Feedback identifies what should become v1.1.
