# motion-plan

## Motion Principle

Motion should communicate polish and responsiveness without turning the template pack into a showpiece.

## Recommended Motion

- Template cards fade in on scroll.
- Preview frames have subtle hover elevation.
- CTA buttons have clear hover/focus states.
- Deployment steps reveal sequentially.

## Avoid

- Heavy animations.
- 3D previews unless already implemented cleanly.
- Decorative blobs or excessive gradients.
- Motion that makes templates harder to inspect.

## Reduced Motion

Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` by disabling transforms and reveals.
