# visual-brief

## Visual Direction

Premium launch command system: sharp, structured, calm, commercially serious, and concrete.

## Palette

- Primary Ink: `#101114`
- Deep Graphite: `#20242B`
- Signal Blue: `#2F6BFF`
- Electric Mint: `#40D9A3`
- Soft Cloud: `#F5F7FA`
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Line Gray: `#D9DEE7`

## Typography

- Headings: Inter Tight or Satoshi.
- Body: Inter.
- Accent labels: IBM Plex Mono.

## Layout

- Mobile-first.
- One primary CTA above the fold.
- Strong section bands rather than nested cards.
- Radius at 8px or less.
- Tables for deliverables and pricing.
- Process strips for the seven-skill workflow.

## Imagery

Use concrete launch artifacts:

- focused founder workspace
- strategy boards
- launch dashboards
- page wireframes
- content calendars
- close crops of notes, screens, and planning material

Avoid robot faces, neon cyberpunk visuals, purple gradients, abstract blobs, and generic corporate stock images.
