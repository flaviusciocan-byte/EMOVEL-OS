# launch-plan

## Goal

Validate EMOVEL Launch Stack v1 as the first monetizable EMOVEL product by selling the Core offer to founders and operators who need a launch-ready product package.

## Funnel

Warm organic traffic and manual outreach move to a direct landing page, then checkout, intake, delivery, premium upsell, and testimonial request.

## Offer Ladder

- Starter: $197 template and prompt kit.
- Core: $497 complete Launch Stack for one idea.
- Premium: $1,500 implementation and review sprint.
- Agency: $3,000 white-label delivery license.

## Pre-Launch

- T-14: positioning post about launch stacks.
- T-12: problem post about unclear products.
- T-10: behind-the-scenes skill system post.
- T-8: direct outreach to 20 warm contacts.
- T-6: sample output.
- T-4: pricing and offer post.
- T-2: pre-launch email or DM follow-up.
- T-1: launch reminder.

## Launch Day

Announce that EMOVEL Launch Stack v1 is live. Explain that it turns a raw product idea into an audience snapshot, offer stack, pricing map, landing page copy, visual brief, funnel map, and 14-day launch plan.

Founding price: $497 for the first 25 buyers.

## Week 1 Metrics

- 50 warm outreach messages.
- 10 replies.
- 250 landing page visits.
- 3 Core sales.
- 1 Premium upsell.
- $2,991 target revenue.
