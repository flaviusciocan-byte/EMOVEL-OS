# motion-plan

## Motion Principle

Use motion to communicate system structure. Keep it restrained and functional.

## Recommended Motion

- Hero text fade-in.
- Prompt system anatomy blocks reveal in order.
- Included system cards get subtle hover states.
- Pricing CTA has clear hover feedback.
- Quality checklist can reveal as a final confidence cue.

## Avoid

- Chat bubble gimmicks.
- Robot or AI sparkle effects.
- Complex animated diagrams that distract from the offer.
- Motion that hides copy.

## Reduced Motion

Disable nonessential transitions when `prefers-reduced-motion` is enabled.
