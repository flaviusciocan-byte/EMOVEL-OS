# Offer: AI Instagram Content OS for Solo Founders

## Product Promise

AI Instagram Content OS helps solo founders turn scattered product thoughts into a repeatable Instagram content system: positioning, content pillars, post prompts, carousel angles, Reel hooks, weekly planning, and launch copy that can be reused every week.

## Target Buyer

Solo founders, indie hackers, consultants, coaches, creators, and service operators who know they should post consistently on Instagram but keep losing time to blank-page planning, random ideas, and content that does not connect back to an offer.

## Core Problem

Most solo founders do not need more content ideas. They need a compact operating system that connects:

- what they sell
- who they sell to
- what their audience already cares about
- what to post this week
- how each post moves people toward trust, replies, waitlist signups, calls, or purchases

## Core Transformation

Before: a founder opens Instagram with vague ideas, saves a few competitor posts, writes something in a rush, and disappears again.

After: the founder has a 30-day content map, reusable AI prompts, daily post briefs, launch post templates, and a review checklist that keeps every post tied to a business goal.

## Primary Deliverable

A downloadable Notion/Markdown-ready content operating system containing:

- Instagram positioning worksheet
- audience and offer clarity prompts
- 5 content pillar generator
- 30-day content calendar
- carousel post prompt pack
- Reel hook and script prompt pack
- story sequence templates
- weekly planning checklist
- launch week content plan
- content QA checklist
- Gumroad-ready product usage guide
