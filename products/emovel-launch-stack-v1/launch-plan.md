# Launch Plan

## Launch Goal

Validate EMOVEL Launch Stack v1 as the first monetizable EMOVEL product by selling the Core offer to founders and operators who need a launch-ready product package.

## Funnel Type

Direct sales page with warm organic traffic and manual outreach.

Funnel map:

```
Founder content / direct outreach / warm network
  -> Landing page
  -> Checkout
  -> Intake form
  -> Launch Stack delivery
  -> Premium implementation upsell
  -> Testimonial / case study request
```

## Offer Ladder

| Stage | Offer | Price | Purpose |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Entry | Starter prompt and template kit | $197 | Capture price-sensitive buyers |
| Core | Complete Launch Stack for one idea | $497 | Main monetizable product |
| Premium | Implementation and review sprint | $1,500 | Higher-touch revenue |
| Agency | White-label license | $3,000 | B2B expansion |

## Conversion Benchmarks

| Funnel Step | Target |
|---|---:|
| Landing page visitor to checkout click | 8% to 15% |
| Checkout click to purchase | 25% to 45% |
| Warm outreach reply rate | 15% to 30% |
| Sales page conversion, warm traffic | 2% to 6% |
| Premium upsell take rate | 10% to 20% |

## Traffic Strategy

### Cold

Do not prioritize cold paid ads for v1. The product needs proof, examples, and language refinement first.

### Warm

Primary channel for first sales.

- Founder network
- Existing collaborators
- LinkedIn audience
- X/Twitter build-in-public audience
- Indie hacker and creator communities
- Agency owners who sell launch or web services

### Hot

- People who previously asked for help packaging a product
- Builders with stalled product ideas
- Consultants with service-to-product ambitions
- Agencies that need repeatable launch strategy assets
